WHY BECOME A CAMPUS AMBASSADOR ?


Be a leader

Improve your management skills and enhance your image. Since you would be representing CUSAT, you would be responsible for certain duties. This will improve your managerial and marketing skills, that would boost your personal growth. Furthermore we would provide you with the necessary experience that would make you more likely to be accepted for similar events.

Feed your inquisitiveness

To improve your skill set, explore new opportunities.(To stay up to date and tuned in to recent trends in science and technology) This event is a magnificent amalgamation of events presented by seven branches, each representing the best advancements in their field, hence there would be no shortage of new technologies to learn about. It would indeed be an enlightening experience.

Boost your Creativity

This position would require you to think outside the box, and get your creative juices flowing. We encourage students to come up with innovative methods to popularize Dhishna. The students could come up with various ideas in multifarious fields of the event, be it related to the technical part or be it aesthetic.

RESPONSIBITITES




PUBLICISE

Spread awareness about Dhishna events and competitions in your respective colleges. Ensure that the students are aware of both the technical as well as non technical events.



IDEATE

Strategise and drive innovative word of mouth campaigns in college activities and events. Inform all the students about the various events that cater to various audiences.



PROMOTE

Ensure enthusiastic participation from your college at Dhishna. Encourage participation, especially group participation.



GUIDELINES FOR CAMPUS AMBASSADOR PROGRAM



INSTAGRAM


  • 1. Each campus ambassador will be provided with a unique referral code , for each invite through the referral code, for making people follow our insta page, you will get 30 points . The person must continue to follow the page at least until Dhishna '20 main event, else 40 points will be deducted.
  • 2. For posting in Instagram as your Instagram post you will be awarded 50 points.
  • 3. For the points to be counted the post should stay up for atleast 2 days.
  • 4. For every story put up at least 10 points will be awarded.
  • 5. For every 50 views 10 points will be awarded and for every 150 views another additional 10 points will be awarded. eg: if your story gets 30 views you will get 10 points 50 views you will get 10 points 100 views you will get 20 points and so on 150 views you will get 30+10=40 points.




WHATSAPP


  • 1. For every group in which sharing is done, 10 points will be awarded.
  • 2. For every fifth group in which sharing is done an additional 10 points is awarded. eg: 4 groups means 40 points and 5 groups mean 60 points.
  • 3. Sharing via whatsaap status has the same point structure as sharing via instagram stories.
